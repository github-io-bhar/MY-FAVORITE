About

Chess is one of the oldest and most popular board games. It is played by two opponents on a checkered board with specially designed pieces of contrasting colours, commonly white and black. The objective of the game is to capture the opponent's king. Chess is a game played between two opponents on opposite sides of a board containing 64 squares of alternating colors.
